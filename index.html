On the left side you can see an interactive map. Hover over a country of interest.
On the right you can choose between two different modes

Importance in Life:
People in the different countries has rated the importance of the different variables. These answers gives a value between 1 and 100 where 100 is very important and 0 is unimportant. With this tool you can see how important the people of the different countries think a certain variable is on average.
You can also compare two variables by choosing a second variable on the right side. This way you can see what variable the people think is more important on average.

Other Questions:
Here you can find a few other questions that may be of interest.
These questions are not compareable.
